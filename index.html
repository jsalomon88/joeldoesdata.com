05

Decide

Insight → Action

Data starts in its raw form — transactional databases, API responses, event streams, exported files. My job begins by understanding what exists, where it lives, and what it actually means to the business.

Transformation is where messy becomes meaningful. Modelling with dbt, testing assumptions in SQL, documenting lineage — so anyone downstream can trust what they're looking at.

Storage is about making the right data cheap and fast to query. Snowflake and BigQuery are the warehouses I reach for most, tuned to the workload.

Good visualization is a teaching act. Looker and Sigma give stakeholders the right level of depth, the right defaults, and the right edges to explore from.

The win isn't the dashboard. It's the decision it unblocks — and how quickly the next one comes.
