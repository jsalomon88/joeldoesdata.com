04 How I work
Source DBs · APIs · Events
Transform dbt · SQL
Store Snowflake · BigQuery
Visualize Looker · Sigma · BI
Decide Insight → Action

Data starts in its raw form — transactional databases, API responses, event streams, exported files. My job begins by understanding what exists, where it lives, and what it actually means to the business.

SQL and dbt turn raw data into clean, tested, documented models. Business logic is centralized and version-controlled — not scattered across BI expressions or one-off queries that nobody else can trust.

Snowflake or BigQuery as the single source of truth. Structured for analytical performance, organized by domain, and accessible to anyone on the team who needs to build on top of it.

Looker, Sigma, or custom dashboards surface the right metrics to the right people. Good visualization is about removing friction between clean data and the person who needs to act on it.

Stakeholders see clean data, understand the story, and take action. A data pipeline only has value when it changes how a business operates — that's the measure of whether the work was worth doing.
