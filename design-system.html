07System rules Non-negotiable
01
Only one accent color per site. It gets used for status, active, and emphasis — never for decoration.
02
Section labels are always mono 10–11px uppercase with 0.16em tracking, prefixed with a two-digit index.
03
Display serif is reserved for hero, statement, and big numbers. Never body copy. Body is always sans.
04
Numbers in tables, telemetry, and data viz use mono with tabular-nums. Big stats use display serif.
05
Cards are 1px hairline border, 4% white fill, 4px radius. Hover raises fill to 6% and border to 18%.
06
Backgrounds follow site warmth: joel true-black, jtmeals warm-near-black (#0B0805), caliper cool-near-black (#060709).
07
Page transitions: fade 280ms in and out between internal links. Scroll-reveal on section entry, 800ms out-cubic.
08
Custom cursor + ring on desktop. Ring grows over interactive elements and shifts to accent color.
